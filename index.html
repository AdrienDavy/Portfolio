ABOUT ME

PASSIONATE ABOUT VFX, DIGITAL ART, WEB TECHNOLOGIES. I LOVE WORKING AT THE INTERSECTION OF IMAGINATION, ART AND WEB SOFT WARES. WHERE POSSIBILITIES ARE BEYOND CREATIVE BOUNDARIES.

WHEN I’M NOT WATCHING TUTORIALS ABOUT SOFTWARES OR NEW WEB TECH, I’M PLAYING VIDEOS GAMES OR DO HIKING AND FILMING LANDSCAPES.

I USE SOME OF THESES SOFTWARES TO SHAPE MY CREATIVITY AND I INTEND TO LEARN MORE ABOUT OTHER LIKE HOUDINI OR UNREAL ENGINE BECAUSE I LIKE TO BE MULTI TASKS.

NOWADAYS, TECHNOLOGIES AND METHODOLOGIES CHANGE IN AN EXPONENTIAL WAY. IT’S A FAST TIME CHANGING GAME WHERE DIGITAL STANDBY IS NOT NEGLIGIBLE. WE MUST ACQUIRE ADAPTABILITY TO FOLLOW THE MOVE.
